Sumo, Fire & Izakaya - Walking food & drink tour - Tokyo - Monzen Nakacho

Decription
Tokyo afternoon / evening walking food and drink tour packed full of things to see, eat and drink. Visit the birthplace of Sumo at the famous local shrine and see for yourself just how big a sumo can be. Watch the Buddhist monks perform their daily "Fire ceremony" at the local temple.

Eat and drink at a famous izakaya (bar) and try the dishes that have attracted international film stars to this local part of town. Choose your favourite sake from the wall to wall refrigerators at the cosy stand up sake izakaya. Finish the night off in the most Japanese of ways with a hearty bowl of delicious local Ramen.

Enjoy five authentic dishes with two alcoholic drinks (non alcoholic drinks are available).
