Sumo Sized Night - Walking food & drink tour - Tokyo - Monzen Nakacho

Decription
Street food at it's best. Tokyo night walking food and drink tour. Visit the birthplace of Sumo at the famous local shrine to summon a sumo sized appetite. Take in the very local vibe. Eat and drink at a famous izakaya (bar) to spot the pictures of international stars.

Find your favourite sake from the wall to wall refidgerators at the stand up sake izakaya. Finish the night off the Japanese way with deliciously cooked rice.

Eat a minimum of 6 different dishes (2 in each establishment) with one drink at each establishment (non alcoholic drinks are available please select the non alcohol ticket).
